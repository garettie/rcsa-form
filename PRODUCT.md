# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Department managers and employees assigned by managers, logging operational risks when prompted by risk officers during quarterly or annual RCSA cycles. Risk officers and compliance auditors also review submitted assessments.

## Product Purpose

An internal tool for banking operational risk self-assessment. Users log risks, score likelihood and impact, evaluate controls, compute inherent/residual risk levels, and track action plans. Exists to streamline regulatory compliance and make the RCSA process systematic rather than ad-hoc.

## Brand Personality

Calm · Methodical · Thorough

Voice is precise and professional without being formal. Reduces the anxiety inherent in compliance workflows through clarity and predictability.

## Anti-references

No specific anti-references provided. Default avoidance of generic SaaS-cream templates, banking clichés (navy + gold, heavy gradients), and flashy/trendy UI patterns that would undermine trust.

## Design Principles

1. Calm over alert — Risk management is anxiety-inducing by nature. The interface should reduce tension, not amplify it. Quiet confidence in layout, restrained color, unhurried interactions.

2. Guide without handholding — The form is the workflow. Section headers, computed scores, and logical grouping make the next step obvious without tooltips or overlays.

3. Data as feedback — Risk scores are not outputs to hunt for; they are signals. Computed values (inherent risk, controls rating, residual risk) feel immediate and informative, not hidden at the bottom of the form.

4. Trustworthy consistency — Banking-grade reliability means every field, table column, and status badge behaves predictably. No surprises, no layout shifts, no ambiguous states.
